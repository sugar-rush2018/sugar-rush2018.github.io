Hotel Information

The LOC provides three hotel options: Jianguo Hotel, New East Asia Hotel and Ji Hotel.

-- Jianguo and New East Asia Hotel are located in Xuhui Central Business District,which is one of the most popular
     downtown hubs of Shanghai, and very convenient for visiting other tourist areas like People’s Square, Xin Tian Di
     and the Bund by Subway. Shuttle bus will be arranged for the 30-min commute between Jianguo/New East Asia
     hotel and the conference venue.
-- Ji Hotel is located in the Minhang District, within walking distance from the Tsung-Dao Lee Institute (~ 1.2km).

More information can be found below.

-- Please note that you will pay the hotel when you check out.
-- DO NOT prepay online since online price is higher than the discount price that LOC provides.
-- To guarantee enough rooms available for our attendees please log in registration system and make your
    hotel option as soon as possible, the deadline is April 20.
