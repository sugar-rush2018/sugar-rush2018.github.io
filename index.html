Studying the Universe with GAlaxy suRveys

Revealing the Unlimited in ShangHai


June 11-15, 2018     Shanghai, China

 

The international conference SUGAR-RUSH will focus on large-scale structure from ongoing and future galaxy surveys, including

* latest results from ongoing surveys;
* opportunities, challenges, and synergies with next-generation galaxy surveys;
* simulation and computational development for the forthcoming data sets;
* statistical and theoretical modeling of galaxy distribution and galaxy-halo connection.

The conference will bring together survey scientists and theorists to foster discussions, aiming at maximizing the scientific returns from galaxy surveys.
